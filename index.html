MCQS Extractor

Extract and format multiple choice questions from text

How to Use

  • Enter your MCQs in the textarea below
  • Each question should end with a question mark (?)
  • Each option should be on a new line
  • Mark the correct answer with an asterisk (*) at the end
  • Separate different questions with an empty line

Example Format

Format your MCQs as shown below:

What is the capital of France? Paris* London Berlin Madrid Which planet is known as the Red Planet? Venus Mars* Jupiter Saturn
